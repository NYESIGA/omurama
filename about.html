About Omurama

Building the Future Through Engineering & Innovation

Omurama Civil and Computer Engineering Company Limited is a multidisciplinary engineering and technology company providing innovative solutions in civil engineering, construction, artificial intelligence, big data analytics, software development, cybersecurity, networking, and digital transformation. We combine engineering excellence, research, and modern technology to deliver sustainable solutions that create lasting impact across Uganda and Africa.

Who We Are

Founded in 2021, Omurama was established with a vision of creating world-class engineering and technology solutions that solve real-world challenges. Our team consists of engineers, software developers, researchers, and innovators committed to delivering high-quality infrastructure and intelligent digital systems.

Through our Civil Engineering, Computer Engineering, and Research & Development divisions, we help governments, organizations, businesses, and communities embrace innovation while achieving sustainable growth.
