Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Omurama Civil and Computer Engineering Company Limited and our professional technology and engineering services.

What services does Omurama provide?

Omurama provides Artificial Intelligence solutions, Big Data Analytics, Software Development, Website Development, Cybersecurity, Cloud Computing, Computer Networking, CCTV Systems, Data Engineering, IT Consultancy, and Civil Engineering services.

Do you develop custom software solutions?

Yes. We design and develop custom web applications, desktop systems, mobile applications, enterprise solutions, and management systems tailored to the specific needs of organizations and businesses.

Can Omurama build professional websites?

Absolutely. We create responsive, secure, fast, and modern websites for businesses, government institutions, NGOs, schools, healthcare facilities, and startups.

What AI and Data Engineering services do you offer?

Our team develops AI-powered systems, machine learning models, intelligent assistants, predictive analytics solutions, data warehouses, ETL/ELT pipelines, and real-time big data processing platforms.

Do you provide cybersecurity services?

Yes. Our cybersecurity services include security assessments, network protection, vulnerability testing, security monitoring, policy development, and cybersecurity awareness training.

Do you offer networking and infrastructure solutions?

Yes. We provide network design, structured cabling, wireless deployments, server installations, data center solutions, and IT infrastructure management services.

Which industries do you serve?

We serve government institutions, healthcare organizations, educational institutions, agriculture, finance, security agencies, NGOs, private companies, and startups across Uganda and beyond.

Do you provide technical support after project completion?

Yes. We offer maintenance services, software updates, system monitoring, technical support, and staff training to ensure smooth operation after project delivery.

How can I request a quotation or consultation?

Contact our team through phone, email, WhatsApp, or the contact form on our website. We will assess your requirements and prepare a detailed proposal and quotation.

Why choose Omurama?

Omurama combines expertise in Artificial Intelligence, Data Engineering, Software Development, Cybersecurity, and Civil Engineering to deliver innovative, reliable, and scalable solutions that help organizations achieve their strategic goals.
