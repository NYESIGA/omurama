What We Do

Omurama Civil and Computer Engineering Company Limited is a technology and engineering company specializing in Artificial Intelligence (AI), Big Data Analytics, Software Development, Data Engineering, Computer Vision, Cybersecurity, Cloud Computing, and Networking Solutions. We help businesses, organizations, and government institutions leverage innovative technologies to improve efficiency, enhance decision-making, and accelerate digital transformation. In addition to technology services, Omurama provides Civil Engineering, Construction, Research, and Development solutions. Our team designs and delivers reliable, scalable, and sustainable systems and infrastructure that address real-world challenges while supporting innovation, economic growth, and national development across Uganda and Africa..
